FIGMA ✷ WIREFRAMING


While going for a hike in my local area with a friend, I came across a beautiful floral plant. It was a shrub of some sort with bright, small fuschia flowers. Naturally, wanting to seem like a plant genius I wanted to be able to name it's genus and species. I wanted to be able to photograph the image, and then quickly save a bunch of useful information.

Even with tools like Google Lens, it felt like there wasn't a clear place I could store all my newfound plant knowledge. That was why I decided to build BioBook, a digital field guide with AI Image recognition and customized lists to collect plants in your local area!

The project is still in the works, but to visualize how it would look and feel I created a simple Figma wireframe design. This wireframe demo shows what a custom list would look like for a user of the platform adding in the tartarian honeysuckle I had seen.
