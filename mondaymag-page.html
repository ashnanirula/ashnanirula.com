in one line:

TheMondayMag is a seasonal mixed media magazine celebrating the magic in the everyday mundane.

in multiple lines:

We are in the golden age of digital media - but so often all that we see are curated, cultivated fragments of real experience. As two optimists and creatives, my co-founder Emily and I hope to create the MondayMag into a unique publication that instead spotlights the mundane aspects of daily life, in a colourful, approachable, and energizing fashion.

Instead of waiting for Friday, what if we appreciated how many beautiful opportunities good ol’ Monday can hold? This question is one we want to answer enthusiastically with pictures, art, and thoughtfully made writing pieces through this medium.

This ongoing project is a labour of love because it has taught me a multitude of skills, from technical web development to creative writing and design. Because we created every minuscule detail from complete scratch, ranging from the logo to the colour scheme, it truly feels like an entity that represents an idea I proudly stand for: viewing life from a state of abundance versus lack.
