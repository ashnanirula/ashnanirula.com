AI ✷ COMPUTER VISION


This project was a replica of Nemo's Garden, the world's first underwater farm. The idea behind it was the fact that since more than half of arable land is used for crops, why not turn to the ocean as a viable surface area for food production?

Through the designing of this prototype I successfully grew a marigold underwater using the principles of hydroponics, water pressure and a repurposed fish tank.
